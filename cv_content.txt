RESEARCH INTERESTS
• Trapped ion system

Previous work: 1D,2D,3D crystal trajectories mathematical and numerical simulation, geometric phase gate
schemes, ion transportation method, suitable species choices.
My new ideas: 1. A CZ gate based on triggering ions motion when one ion moving inside cavity. 2. Hybird system of
ions and atoms. 3. Kicking ions outside Lamb-Dicke limit for distant, scalable connection.
• Rydberg atom platform

Previous work: Numerical simulation, laser implementation, possible gate schemes, ion transportation method,
suitable species choices.
My new ideas: 1. A possible scheme to connect atoms outside one Rydberg blockade radius. 2. Hybird system of
ions and atoms.
• Non-Hermiticity in superconducting qubits

Previous work: 3-qubit and 4-qubit gate design in non-Hermitain all-to-all connected superconducting qubits,
accelerating entanglement compared with Hermitian regime, Pulse shaping in non-Hermitian system.
• Cavity QED system

Previous work: Gate design utilising spatial dependent Tavis-Cummings model.
My new ideas: 1. Combination with trapped ion normal modes and cavity mode for a CZ gate. → Analysis of
coupling between phonon and photon might be interesting.
• Quantum computing and quantum information

Previous work: Decomposing an entangled state using an entangling gate, which I designed in a superconducting
qubits non-Hermitian system. This gate functions as a combination of two consecutive controlled-controlled-Z gates.
