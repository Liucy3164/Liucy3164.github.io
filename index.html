Research Interests

Trapped Ion Systems

Previous work: 1D, 2D, 3D crystal trajectories mathematical and numerical simulation, geometric phase gate schemes, ion transportation methods, suitable species choices.

New ideas: CZ gate based on triggering ion motion when one ion moves inside cavity; hybrid system of ions and atoms; kicking ions outside Lamb-Dicke limit for distant, scalable connection.

Rydberg Atom Platform

Previous work: Numerical simulation, laser implementation, possible gate schemes, ion transportation methods, suitable species choices.

New ideas: Scheme to connect atoms outside one Rydberg blockade radius; hybrid system of ions and atoms.

Non-Hermiticity in Superconducting Qubits

Previous work: 3-qubit and 4-qubit gate design in non-Hermitian all-to-all connected superconducting qubits, accelerating entanglement compared with Hermitian regime, pulse shaping in non-Hermitian systems.

Cavity QED System

Previous work: Gate design utilizing spatial dependent Tavis-Cummings model.

New ideas: Combination with trapped ion normal modes and cavity mode for a CZ gate. Analysis of coupling between phonon and photon.

Quantum Computing & Information

Previous work: Decomposing entangled states using entangling gates designed in superconducting qubits non-Hermitian systems. Gate functions as combination of two consecutive controlled-controlled-Z gates.
